What is FightOut?

FightOut is building a fitness app and high-end gym chain. At its heart, FightOut is a Move-to-Earn platform that rewards users for completing workouts whether in the gym or at home in their living room. User workouts are personalised to their goals and ability, and their progress and achievements are analysed and reflected directly in their digital avatars attributes.

Why FightOut?

When combat athletes compete, we are seeing the end result of their training. Only part of their regimen is fighting technique. In order to excel, combat athletes achieve a high level of cardio, strength, muscular endurance, and wellness.

That said, fighters who achieve a high baseline across attributes but excel in specific areas can have massive competitive advantages.

We have used these principles to form our FightOut workouts and shaped our Move-to-Earn mechanisms to reward the FightOut community for a holistic approach to fitness.

Why Move-to-Earn?

Move-to-Earn Web3 projects typically lack mainstream adoption due to requiring large upfront financial investments and Web3 knowledge. As FightOuts Move-to-Earn mechanism has been specifically designed to avoid these pitfalls, it will allow FightOut to differentiate itself in the market and be a powerful user acquisition strategy contributing to the success of the project and upward pressure on the value of $FGHT.

How can I buy $FGHT?

$FGHT can be purchased on FightOut.com using ETH, USDT, or via credit card through our partner Transak.

With the Transak widget you need to purchase ETH using your card, then swap for $FGHT. Ensure you have enough ETH to buy $FGHT and cover the gas fees.

REPS vs $FGHT, whats the difference?

$FGHT is our native token, and can be bought in our presale from FightOut.com.

REPS is our in-app currency, earned by completing workouts and training regimes. REPS can then be used to purchase items in the FightOut app, including cosmetics and power-ups for your FightOut avatar.

What is the FightOut avatar?

App users will be able to mint their own NFT avatar when creating a FightOut account. This avatar is soulbound, meaning it cannot be sold or transferred.

The data collected from IRL workouts are converted directly into stat points for your avatar.

Your avatar will be your digital self in the FightOut metaverse, where youll be able to socialise with other members of the FightOut community and compete against others for prizes.
